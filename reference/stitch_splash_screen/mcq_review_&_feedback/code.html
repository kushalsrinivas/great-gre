Question 01 check_circle

The senator's speech was surprisingly ______, lacking the expected rhetorical flourish.

expand_more
Your Answer Laconic

Explanation

The contrast with 'rhetorical flourish' suggests a need for a word meaning brief or concise. 'Laconic' fits this context perfectly, as it describes a style of speaking that uses few words to express complex ideas.

Question 02 cancel

Her ______ nature made it difficult for her to make friends in the new city.

expand_more
Your Answer Gregarious
Correct Diffident

Explanation

The phrase "difficult for her to make friends" suggests a personality trait that involves shyness or a lack of self-confidence. Diffident specifically refers to modesty or shyness arising from a lack of self-confidence. Gregarious would mean the exact opposite.
