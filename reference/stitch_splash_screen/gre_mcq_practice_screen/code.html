Question 12

In the context of modern international relations, the diplomat's approach was surprisingly ______, often eschewing traditional protocol in favor of direct, if sometimes abrasive, communication.

info

Detailed Explanation

The sentence describes a diplomat who "eschews traditional protocol" and uses "direct, abrasive communication." This behavior is the opposite of conventional diplomatic norms.

  • Unorthodox (Correct): Means contrary to what is usual, traditional, or accepted. This fits the description of ignoring protocol.
  • Circumspect (Trap): Means wary and unwilling to take risks, which contradicts the "abrasive" and "direct" description.
